Action Plan

This is fairly straightforward. Write out on a sheet of paper five measurable things that you will do. The first two are already set. The rest is up to you.

  • Go to AA meetings
  • Find a sponsor
  • Plans in work/life after starting this process..
    • Do you want to go to treatment?
    • How do you plan on making up any shortcomings at work?
    • Do you plan on involving any of your family or friends in this process?
